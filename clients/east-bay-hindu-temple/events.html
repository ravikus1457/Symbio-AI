Events & Programs

There is always something happening at the temple — weekly satsang, festivals, music and language classes, and seva for every age. Everyone is welcome.

Hanuman Chalisa & Ramayana Path

Join us and your family every Tuesday evening for bhajan, kirtan, and aarti — sing the glories of Lord Hanuman, receive his blessings, and enjoy the spiritual atmosphere. Prasad is served for all. जय श्री राम · जय हनुमान।

For every generation

Programs for All Ages

From our youngest devotees to our elders, there is a place for everyone to learn, serve, and celebrate together.

Music Classes

Vocal and instrumental classes — learn bhajan, kirtan, tabla, and harmonium in the devotional tradition.

Hindi Language Classes

Reading and writing Hindi for children and adults — keeping our language and scriptures alive for the next generation.

Youth & Cultural Activities

Festivals, garba, cultural programs, and youth gatherings that bring families together across generations.

Bhajan & Kirtan

Weekly devotional singing — lend your voice, or simply come and be uplifted by the sound of the divine.

Annadanam · Food Seva

Cooking and serving Maha Prasad to the community — feeding others is among the highest forms of devotion.

Poojas & Havans

Daily worship and ceremonies for every occasion. See the full list and prices on our Poojas & Prices page.
