Welcome · स्वागतम्

A temple built by the community, for the community

East Bay Hindu Temple is a non-profit place of worship serving Hindu families across Pittsburg and the greater East Bay. We are dedicated to keeping our traditions alive — through daily worship, the teaching of our scriptures, and joyful festivals that bring families together across generations.

Whether you are visiting for a quiet moment of darshan, booking a pooja for your home, or celebrating a festival with the whole community, you will find a warm welcome here. Everyone is welcome, regardless of background.

Sevas · सेवा

Poojas & Sevas

Our priest performs poojas at the temple and at your home for every occasion — from a child's naming to a new home, a wedding, or a memorial. Call to book and discuss sankalp.

Archana & Daily Pooja

Offer archana in your name and gotra at the feet of the deities, with prasad to take home.

Abhishekam & Havan

Sacred abhishekam and havan / homam for health, prosperity, and the fulfilment of vows.

Griha Pravesh & Home Visits

House-warming, Vastu pooja, and home ceremonies — our priest will visit your home on request.

Weddings & Sanskaars

Vivah (wedding), Namkaran (naming), Mundan, and the sixteen sanskaars performed with full vidhi.

Satyanarayan Katha

Monthly and on-request Satyanarayan pooja and katha for the well-being of your family.

Memorial & Shanti Pooja

Antim sanskaar rites, shraddh, and shanti poojas performed with care and compassion.
