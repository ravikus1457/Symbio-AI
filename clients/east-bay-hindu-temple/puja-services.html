Sevas · सेवा

Poojas, Sevas & Prices

Every ceremony below is performed by our head priest, Pandit Rakesh Bhargav ji, with full vidhi — at the temple or in your home. Prices are shown clearly, with what each pooja means and whether supplies are included.

How our pricing works

Most poojas are shown as three parts: Pandit Dakshina (the priest's offering), a Temple contribution that keeps the mandir running, and Puja Supplies (samagri). The “with supplies” total includes all materials — if you prefer to bring your own samagri, just let us know when you book and the supplies amount is waived. Every ceremony can be performed at the temple or at your home.
